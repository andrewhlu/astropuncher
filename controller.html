Captain! Your ship is under attack!

You must defend your ship at all costs! Your ship mate is outside defending incoming asteroids. But you must launch your own to attack the enemy!

Swipe up from the Play Area to launch asteroids at the enemy ship. But keep in mind that the ship can only generate so much power at once!

Stronger asteroids (longer swipes) consume more energy.

You are also in a battlefield where asteroids can bounce off walls! Use that to your advantage.
